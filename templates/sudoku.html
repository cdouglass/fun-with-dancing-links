How to play

Your goal: to fill every row, column, and 3x3 subgrid with exactly one of each of the digits 1 through 9.

To fill a square, click on or tab to it, then enter a digit. Right click to clear. To see if you've made a mistake and entered the same number twice in the same row, column, or subgrid, click "Hint".
